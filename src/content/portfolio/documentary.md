An intimate documentary exploring social change through the voices of local activists. This project required careful pacing, emotional storytelling, and seamless integration of archival footage.

**Challenges addressed:**
- 20+ hours of interview footage
- Archival footage restoration
- Subtle motion graphics for context
- Sensitive subject matter handling

The documentary was selected for a local film festival and praised for its compassionate storytelling.
